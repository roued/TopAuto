The new Lamborghini Centenario speaks to another, greatly valuable piece in Lamborghini's coincidental methodology. It is an ideal case of the creative outline and the building aptitudes of the bull-marked maker.The best tribute to the 100th commemoration of the introduction of Mr. Ferruccio Lamborghini, a man who figured out how to make a world-class brand, and who dependably trusted that nothing is inconceivable, consequently delivering incredible and phenomenal vehicles that left a mark on the world for super games autos.

 

Here are the technical characteristics of the Lamborghini Centenario: equipped with a 770 CV aspirated V12 engine springing from 0 to 100 km/h in 2.8 seconds, the newly-born Lamborghini car has been produced in a limited edition, for a total of 40 models: 20 Coupés and 20 Roadsters will be delivered to Lamborghini collectors and fans starting from 2017.The Centenario has been conceived with the purpose of exploring new technological and design opportunities, to look at the future through the lens of innovation. One of the most exclusive (and sought-after) cars in the whole world.
